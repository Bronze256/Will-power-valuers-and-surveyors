Our Journey Since 2012

Founded in 2012, Will Power Valuers & Surveyors began as a small firm with a vision to provide accurate, reliable, and professional property valuation services in Kampala. What started as a two-person operation has grown into one of Uganda's most respected valuation and surveying companies.

Over the past decade, we have expanded our services, grown our team of certified professionals, and established partnerships with financial institutions, government agencies, and international organizations. Our commitment to integrity, accuracy, and client satisfaction has been the cornerstone of our success.

Our Journey Timeline

Key milestones in our growth and development over the past decade.

2012

Company Foundation

Will Power Valuers & Surveyors was established in Kampala with a focus on residential property valuation. Started with 2 staff members and a single office.

2014

First Major Bank Partnership

Secured our first major contract with a leading commercial bank for mortgage valuation services, marking a significant expansion in our client base.

2016

Expansion to Commercial Valuation

Expanded our services to include commercial and industrial property valuation, responding to growing market demand.

2018

Team Growth & Office Relocation

Moved to our current office in Princess Complex and expanded our team to 15 certified professionals. Introduced advanced surveying equipment.

2020

Digital Transformation

Implemented comprehensive digital tools for remote valuation, 3D mapping, and drone surveying technology adoption.

2022

10-Year Anniversary & Recognition

Celebrated 10 years of service with over 2,750 projects completed. Received industry recognition for excellence in property valuation.
