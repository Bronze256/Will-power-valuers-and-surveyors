Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and contact process.

How quickly can you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 0705 839845 for immediate assistance.

What information do I need to provide for a property valuation?

For an initial quote, we need the property location, type (residential/commercial/industrial), size, and purpose of valuation. For a detailed valuation, we'll need property documents, recent photos, and access for inspection.

How long does a property valuation take?

The timeframe depends on the property type and complexity. Standard residential valuations take 2-3 business days, while commercial properties may take 5-7 business days. We'll provide an estimated timeline after the initial assessment.

Do you provide services outside Kampala?

Yes, we provide valuation and surveying services across Uganda. We have teams that can travel to different regions. Travel costs may apply for locations outside the greater Kampala area.

What are your payment terms?

For most services, we require a 50% deposit before commencing work, with the balance due upon completion and delivery of the report. We accept bank transfers, mobile money, and cash payments.
